## Strategic Landscape Summary

### Where the Market Is Heading

1. **AI generation is becoming table stakes.** In 2023, AI flashcard generation was a differentiator. By 2026, Quizlet, Knowt, and RemNote all offer it. The race is now on AI quality (accuracy, formatting, context-awareness) and how well AI integrates with the study workflow.

2. **FSRS is winning the SRS algorithm war.** Anki adopted FSRS natively; Deckbase ships FSRS. Competitors without FSRS (Quizlet, Knowt, Brainscape) face growing criticism for weak spaced repetition as users become more informed.

3. **Mobile-first is replacing desktop-first.** Anki's desktop-first model is being actively replaced by mobile-first apps. The iOS/Android experience increasingly determines adoption among students.
