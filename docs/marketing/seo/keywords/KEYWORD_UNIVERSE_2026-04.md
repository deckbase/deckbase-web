## Top 5 — Start Here

### 1. `ai flashcards generator` — 1,000/mo, KD 14, informational
**Why now:** KD 14 is the lowest difficulty at this volume across the entire universe. Informational intent means a how-to blog post works perfectly — no product page needed.  
**Page type:** Blog post — "How AI Flashcard Generators Work (and How to Use One)"  
**Cluster:** Anchors the AI Flashcard Creation cluster. Cross-link to `/ai-flashcards`.

### 2. `spaced repetition app` — 1,000/mo, KD 29, +171% YoY, commercial
**Why now:** Growing explosively. Deckbase has FSRS built in — most AI flashcard tools don't. This is a genuine differentiator, not a stretch.  
**Page type:** Landing page or long-form blog — "Best Spaced Repetition Flashcard App in 2026"  
**Cluster:** Pillar for Spaced Repetition cluster. Also captures `best spaced repetition app` (KD 12) and `spaced repetition flashcard app` (+336% YoY).

### 3. `free flashcard app` — 1,300/mo, KD 26, commercial
**Why now:** High volume, moderate difficulty, and directly maps to the download/sign-up conversion goal. The free tier is the hook.  
**Page type:** App download page (`/download` or `/flashcard-app`) with clear free tier CTA.  
**Cluster:** Pillar for Flashcard App cluster.

### 4. `anki alternative` — 140/mo, KD ~5, transactional
**Why now:** Lowest KD in the commercial/transactional space. Users searching this are ready to switch — highest conversion probability in the universe.  
**Page type:** `/anki-alternatives` (already exists — update and expand with Deckbase import/export angle).  
**Cluster:** Anchors Anki Alternatives cluster. Cross-link from `/deckbase-vs-anki`.

### 5. `anki import csv` / `anki import` — 110–70/mo, KD 6, navigational→transactional
**Why now:** KD 6. Nobody ranks for this seriously. Deckbase's CSV/spreadsheet import is a direct, literal match. This is a **unique positioning opportunity** no other AI flashcard app is targeting.  
**Page type:** Tutorial/guide — "How to Import Your Anki Decks into Deckbase (CSV, Excel, and More)"  
**Cluster:** Seeds the Anki Import/Export cluster. Shows up-funnel Anki users Deckbase as the natural migration target.
