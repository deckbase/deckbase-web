Deckbase is an AI-powered flashcard platform that helps people turn what they read into lasting knowledge. Users can capture text from articles, PDFs, books, or notes and Deckbase automatically converts it into well-structured flashcards. The app uses spaced repetition to schedule reviews at scientifically optimal intervals, helping users remember what they learn long-term. Key features include AI-powered card generation, multi-source text capture, smart formatting, learning analytics, and deck sharing.
